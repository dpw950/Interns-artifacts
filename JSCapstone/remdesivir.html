Remdesivir

Salt Composition:

Carboxylic ester


About:

Remdesivir is an antiviral medication used to treat severe cases of COVID-19. It inhibits viral replication by targeting the virus's RNA polymerase enzyme, shortening recovery time in hospitalized patients.


Use:

COVID treatment


Side effects:

Nausea, gastrointestinal issues, and elevated liver enzymes.
