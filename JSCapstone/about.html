About XYZPharma

Became a public company in 1987 and is well known for its cost-effective manufacturing capablities and a bunch of customers. The company also entered the high speciality generic formulation segment. the formulation business is regularly originated with a divisional structure and has a focused team for international markets. The formulations are designed to meet the requirements of both advanced as well as emerging market oppurtunities. Being in the top 2 Pharmaceutical companies in India for the consolidated revenues. XYZPharma exports to over 124 countries across the world with around 92% of revenues acquired from the international markets.
