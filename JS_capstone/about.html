About Us

TPT Pharma is the fourth largest specialty generic pharmaceutical company in the world with global revenues of over US$ 5.1 billion. Supported by more than 40 manufacturing facilities, we provide high-quality, affordable medicines, trusted by healthcare professionals and patients, to more than 100 countries across the globe.

Awards

From humble beginnings in 1983, TPT Pharma has grown to become one of the largest generic pharmaceutical companies worldwide. Take a look at the milestones and recognitions in our journey.

  • 2022: We acquired Uractiv portfolio from Fiterman Pharma in Romania. We were recognised as the #1 generic pharmaceutical company by patient groups in the annual PatientView ‘Corporate Reputation of Pharma’ survey 2021
  • 2021: We received the Golden Peacock Award for Corporate Social Responsibility, 2020. We won the Dun & Bradstreet India Corporate Award under the Sectoral Performance-based category- Pharmaceuticals
