Product Details

Cetirizine Syrup I.P.

Price : Rs.20

Composition

Cetirizine (5mg)

Uses

Treatment of Allergic conditions.

Usage

Take this medicine in the dose and duration as advised by your doctor. Check the label for directions before use. Measure it with a measuring cup and take it by mouth. Shake well before use. Cetrizine Syrup may be taken with or without food, but it is better to take it at a fixed time.

Working

Cetrizine Syrup belongs to a class of medicines called antihistamines. It blocks the natural chemical substance (histamine) produced in the body during an allergic state and subsides (lowers) your child’s immune system’s response to these conditions. As a result, the symptoms are relieved, and eventually, the condition is cured.
